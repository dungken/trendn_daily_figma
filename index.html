Car insurance questions

There are important steps you need to take if you ever find yourself in a car accident:
  • Always remember to check you and your passengers for injuries first, then call 911.
  • Exchange info with the other driver and take photos. Try to avoid discussions about who's at fault.
  • Report the accident to your insurance company by filing a claim.

The cost of car insurance per month varies depending on your location, vehicle, age, and driving history.

A deductible is the amount of money you agree to pay out-of-pocket before your insurance kicks in for a covered claim.

Many auto insurance policies extend to rental cars, but you should check your policy to be sure.

Gap insurance covers the difference between the car's actual cash value and the balance still owed on your loan or lease.
